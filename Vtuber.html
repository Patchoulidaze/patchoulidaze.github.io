About Vtubers

What are Vtubers?

Ever wondered about Vtubers?

Vtubers, the short form for Virtual Youtubers, are streamers who use a virtual avatars instead of a real live face stream of themselves while streaming
Their avatars tend to be expressive, with facial, mouth and eye movements, and are mixed in with video effects
2D avatars with such features are usually called "Live 2D" avatars, after a Japanese video technology used to animate static 2D characters
Vtubers became popular in Japan as a way to stay anonymous while streaming, while catering to the anime otaku audience.
With Covid,people were stuck indoors, and naturally turned to watching streams, which increased viewership in Japan
Eventually overseas viewers starting climbing on the Vtuber bandwango, attracted to the uniqueness of vtubers, but
stayed due to the personality of Vtubers

There are 2 big companies in Japan where whose Vtubers are most watched in the world,Nijisanji and HoloLive
