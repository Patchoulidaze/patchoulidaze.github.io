About Us

Who are we?

Min Flavored Mochi is an artist collabration circle that is highly involved in the Vtuber artist community.
We aim to start our own Formal Vtuber group in Singapore, to provide assistance to current as well as anyone
who aims to start their Vtuber careers.
Eventually, we aim to market Vtubers in Singapore and bring more awareness in Singapore on streaming and Vtubers in general,
as well as build up resources for the Singaporean Vtuber community in general
